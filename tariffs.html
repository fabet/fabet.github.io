Our tariffs

Thunderbolt Membership

The Thunderbolt Membership grants you access to the gym during off-peak hours, typically during quieter times of the day. This means you can enjoy a more serene and focused workout experience without the hustle and bustle of peak hours. While you have limited access to group fitness classes, you can still participate in selected sessions to add variety and motivation to your workouts. The membership allows you to make use of the basic gym equipment and facilities available, including cardio machines, weightlifting equipment, and designated workout areas. However, personal training sessions are not included in this membership, so you'll have the opportunity to work out independently and at your own pace.

25$

Lightning Membership

The Lightning Membership provides you with a comprehensive gym experience, allowing you access to the facility during both peak and off-peak hours. This means you have the flexibility to work out whenever it suits your schedule best, whether it's during busy hours or quieter times. With full access to group fitness classes, you can participate in any session that catches your interest, from high-intensity interval training to yoga and dance classes. The membership offers use of advanced gym equipment and facilities, including state-of-the-art cardio machines, weightlifting equipment, functional training areas, and more. Additionally, you'll receive one personal training session per month, where a certified trainer will guide you through a tailored workout, provide expert advice, and help you set and achieve your fitness goals.

50$

Olympus Membership

The Olympus Membership unlocks the doors to unlimited access to the gym, giving you the freedom to work out at any time that suits you, whether it's during peak hours when the energy is high or off-peak hours when you prefer a more relaxed atmosphere. This membership offers full access to all group fitness classes, allowing you to explore a wide range of exercise modalities and find the ones that resonate with your interests and goals. You'll have access to advanced gym equipment and facilities, such as cutting-edge strength training machines, cardio equipment with built-in entertainment features, spacious workout areas, and more. As part of this membership, you'll also receive two complimentary personal training sessions per month, where experienced trainers will develop personalized workout plans, provide one-on-one guidance, and help you optimize your fitness journey.

75$

Titan Membership

The Titan Membership provides an exclusive and premium gym experience. As a Titan member, you'll enjoy VIP access to the gym, granting you entry at any time, even during peak hours. This ensures you can work out without any time constraints or waiting periods. In addition, you'll have priority booking for group fitness classes, allowing you to secure your spot in popular sessions and enjoy a seamless class experience. The membership offers use of premium gym equipment and facilities, including top-of-the-line cardio machines, cutting-edge strength training equipment, dedicated functional training areas, and more. You'll also receive four complimentary personal training sessions per month, where highly qualified trainers will provide individualized attention, customized workout programs, and ongoing support to help you reach your fitness goals. Furthermore, the Titan Membership provides access to exclusive member events, such as workshops, seminars, and fitness challenges, where you can connect with like-minded individuals and further enhance your fitness journey. Additionally, you'll enjoy exclusive discounts on additional services, such as spa treatments, nutrition consultations, and specialized classes, creating a holistic and rewarding membership experience.

100$
