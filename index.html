Our tariffs

Thunderbolt Membership

  • Access to the gym during off-peak hours only.
  • Limited access to group fitness classes.
  • Use of basic gym equipment and facilities.
  • No access to personal training sessions.

Lightning Membership

  • Access to the gym during both peak and off-peak hours.
  • Full access to group fitness classes.
  • Use of advanced gym equipment and facilities.
  • Limited access to one personal training session per month.

Olympus Membership

  • Unlimited access to the gym during both peak and off-peak hours.
  • Full access to all group fitness classes.
  • Use of advanced gym equipment and facilities.
  • Two complimentary personal training sessions per month.

Titan Membership

  • VIP access to the gym, allowing entry at any time.
  • Priority booking for group fitness classes.
  • Use of premium gym equipment and facilities.
  • Four complimentary personal training sessions per month.
  • Access to exclusive member events and discounts on additional services.
